Glass Sheets

Glass sheets and plate glass consist of flat glass materials in the form of thin or wide slab planar structures.We offer Glass sheets and plate glass in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, thicknesses, and with a multitude of physical and chemical characteristics. Glass sheets and plate glass can be clear or transparent, or opalescent or iridescent quality.

Polycarbonate

Polycarbonate roof sheets are made up of polycarbonate polymer which is durable , high impact- resistant , low scratch-resistant heat resistant. Polycarbonate are environment friendly and economical in use. These sheets are good option to use for controlling the sunlight and maintaining the temperature inside the property. These are strong , light , impact resistant and practically unbreakable.
